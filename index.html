Projects

Optimization of Conventional Machine using ML

We have used Machine Learning for Optimization of Electric discharge machine. Using AI Model we have optimized various factor that can increase the overall efficiency of machine such as material intake, feed rate, cost, electric consumptions, etc.

Potato Leaf Disease Classification

In this project, we have used CNN model to predict disease associated with potato leaf and classify it on the basis of its nurturing condition. The user is required to upload the image of leaf and the model can predict weather the condition of crop is poor or good. This project is state-of-art and we have also published research paper for the same.

Design and Fabrication of an ATV

We have designed and fabricted an All-Terrain Vehicle which can surf on any road condition. The vehicle was purposely developed to compete in SAE BAJA INDIA along with 190 other colleges. The vehicle is fabricted from scratch and it has the ability to endure any road condition. Equipped with 4-wheel drive mechanism along with independent suspension, disk-brake with double piston caliper, roll-over protection and many more art-of-the state feature.
